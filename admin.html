Admin Account
Email Forwarding

All emails sent to @devresse.fit are forwarded to thom.devresse@gmail.com

Cloudflare Email Routing (Recommended)

If your domain is on Cloudflare, go to Email → Email Routing → Routes and add:

*@devresse.fit → thom.devresse@gmail.com

This catches all emails: tomas@, contact@, info@, etc.

DNS Records Required

If using a different provider, add these MX records:

MX devresse.fit 10 route1.mx.cloudflare.net

MX devresse.fit 51 route2.mx.cloudflare.net

MX devresse.fit 26 route3.mx.cloudflare.net

Or for Google Workspace forwarding:

MX devresse.fit 1 ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM

Vercel Email (if domain is on Vercel)

Go to Vercel Dashboard → Domain → devresse.fit → Email

Add forwarding rule: *@devresse.fit → thom.devresse@gmail.com
